Arepas

Traditional arepas and a few filling ideas

Think about arepas as a blank canvas made with corn, water, and sabor!('taste' in Spanish)

Arepas can be paired with any other savory food. Try the traditional Colombian arepa with cheese, or arepa with egg. If you feel bold, you can try the more complex Venezualan arepas, with shredded beef, beans, plantain and cheese (just to name a few). Think of arepas as an improved, gluten-free version of bread... yes, arepas are THAT "best thing since slice bread" that people are talking about.
